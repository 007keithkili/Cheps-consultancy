Got Questions? We've Got Answers

Everything you need to know about working with us

Q What is CHEP'S Consultancy?

CHEP'S Consultancy is a professional overseas employment and relocation consultancy dedicated to helping individuals secure international job opportunities and successfully transition to life abroad. We provide guidance throughout the entire process, from job sourcing and application support to visa processing and relocation assistance.

Q How do I start my global career journey with CHEPS?

Simply reach out via WhatsApp or our contact form. We'll schedule a consultation to understand your career goals, qualifications, and preferred destinations. Our team will then guide you through the entire process, from job matching to relocation support.

Q What services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of services including:

  • Overseas Job Placement — Connecting you with reputable international employers
  • Visa Assistance — Expert guidance on work visas and immigration pathways
  • Document Processing — Professional document preparation and verification
  • Relocation Support — Housing, cultural orientation, and settling-in services
  • Interview Coaching — Expert preparation for international job interviews
  • Career Guidance — Personalized counseling for your global career
Q Do you charge a fee for your services?

We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Our services are tailored to each client's needs. Contact us for a detailed consultation and personalized quote based on your specific requirements and destination.

Q What is your visa success rate?

Our visa success rate is 98%. We have expert immigration consultants who guide you through every step of the process, ensuring all documentation is complete and accurate before submission. We stay updated on the latest visa requirements for all our destination countries.

Q How long does the visa process take?

The timeline varies depending on the country, job type, and visa processing times. Typically, the process can take 2-6 months from initial consultation to relocation. We provide regular updates throughout the journey and help expedite the process where possible.

Q Do you offer relocation support?

Yes, we provide comprehensive relocation support including:

  • Housing Assistance — Help finding suitable accommodation
  • Cultural Orientation — Understanding local customs and workplace culture
  • Settling-in Services — Guidance on banking, healthcare, and local systems
  • Ongoing Support — We're here to help even after you arrive
Q What support do you provide after I arrive?

Our support doesn't end when you arrive. We provide ongoing assistance including:

  • Check-ins during your first few months
  • Help with any issues that arise with your employer or accommodation
  • Guidance on local services and systems
  • Connecting you with expat communities and support networks
