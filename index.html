Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services and processes

1. How do I start my global career journey with CHEPS?
Simply reach out via WhatsApp or our contact form. We'll schedule a consultation to understand your career goals, qualifications, and preferred destinations. Our team will then guide you through the entire process.
2. What countries do you recruit for?
We recruit for positions in Netherlands, Dubai (UAE), New Zealand, Ireland, Qatar, Portugal, Serbia, Slovakia, Kuwait, Saudi Arabia, and more. We continuously expand our network of partner employers worldwide.
3. What is your visa success rate?
Our visa success rate is 98%. We have expert immigration consultants who guide you through every step of the process, ensuring all documentation is complete and accurate before submission.
4. Do you offer relocation support?
Yes, we provide comprehensive relocation support including housing assistance, cultural orientation, settling-in services, and guidance on local systems such as banking and healthcare.
5. What types of jobs do you offer?
We offer opportunities across various sectors including Technology, Healthcare, Engineering, Construction, Finance, Business, Hospitality, and more. We work with employers in multiple industries to find the right fit for your skills.
6. How long does the entire process take?
The timeline varies depending on the country, job type, and visa processing times. Typically, the process can take 2-6 months from initial consultation to relocation. We provide regular updates throughout the journey.
7. Do you charge a fee for your services?
We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Our services are tailored to each client's needs. Contact us for a detailed consultation and personalized quote.
